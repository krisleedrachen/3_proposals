3 Proposals

Digital Under Taker

What

Our goal is to craft an experience for users as if they are hiring the digital undertaker

Why

Due to unremovable digital footprints such as revenge porn or distribution of illegal pornography, clients can employ a digital undertaker to clean all traces of them on the internet.

How

We want to provoke and dramatize the emotions which come from the act of entirely erasing everything and letting go.

Multi Identity

What

We wanted to showcase to people the experience of having various identities on the internet.

Why

With the development of the internet, people are no longer confined to a single identity. In other words, on the internet, it has become impossible to know which persona is the user's true self. The internet allows users to indirectly experience the lives of users with diverse personalities and encourages them to explore their authentic selves.

How

Motivate users to explore and personally experience different accounts.
